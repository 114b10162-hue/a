ℹ️ About

This digital clock displays the current time across multiple time zones around the world. You can add custom time zones by selecting a city and timezone, or load preset cities. Click the "Toggle 24/12 Hour" button to switch between 24-hour and 12-hour formats.

Features:

  • Real-time clock updates every second
  • Support for all major world time zones
  • Add custom cities and time zones
  • Toggle between 24-hour and 12-hour formats
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
